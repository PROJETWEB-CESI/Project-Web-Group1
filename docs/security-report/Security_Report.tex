\section*{Overview}

This report consolidates all security vulnerability fixes applied across the
\texttt{fix/security-issues}, \texttt{fix/security-issues-clean}, and related
branches, addressing findings reported by Snyk Open Source and Snyk Code. All
changes listed below have been merged into \texttt{develop} via pull requests
\#53, \#55, and \#71.

\section{Dependency Vulnerabilities}

\small
\begin{longtable}{L{3.0cm}L{3.4cm}L{4.4cm}L{4.8cm}}
\toprule
\textbf{CVE / Snyk ID} & \textbf{Component} & \textbf{Issue} & \textbf{Fix} \\
\midrule
\endhead

SNYK-JS-UUID-16133035 &
\seqsplit{iam-service}, \seqsplit{academic-service}, \seqsplit{billing-service} (transitive via \texttt{sequelize}/\seqsplit{express-validator} $\rightarrow$ \texttt{uuid@8.3.2}) &
Vulnerable \texttt{uuid} version with insecure \texttt{Math.random()}-based fallback &
Pinned via \texttt{npm overrides} to \texttt{uuid@\^{}11.1.1} in all three services, which fully resolves the finding (no \texttt{.snyk} policy needed) \\
\addlinespace

Snyk -- \texttt{bcrypt} \(\rightarrow\) \texttt{tar} &
\texttt{iam-service} &
\texttt{bcrypt@\^{}5.1.1} pulled a vulnerable \texttt{tar} (arbitrary file write / DoS via crafted tarball) &
Bumped \texttt{bcrypt} to \texttt{\^{}6.0.0} \\
\addlinespace

Snyk -- \texttt{geoip-lite} \(\rightarrow\) \texttt{inflight}, \texttt{ip-address} &
\texttt{iam-service} &
\texttt{geoip-lite@\^{}1.4.10} pulled vulnerable \texttt{inflight} (memory leak) and \texttt{ip-address} (ReDoS) &
Bumped \texttt{geoip-lite} to \texttt{\^{}2.0.2} \\
\addlinespace

CVE-2026-41305 &
All frontend / Next.js workspaces &
Next.js vendors \texttt{postcss@8.4.31}, vulnerable to XSS via CSS stringify output &
\texttt{npm overrides} forcing \texttt{postcss >=8.5.10}, deduping to patched \texttt{postcss@8.5.15} \\

\bottomrule
\end{longtable}

\section{Code-Level (Snyk Code) Findings}

\small
\begin{longtable}{L{3.4cm}L{3.6cm}L{4.1cm}L{4.5cm}}
\toprule
\textbf{Snyk Rule} & \textbf{Location} & \textbf{Issue} & \textbf{Fix} \\
\midrule
\endhead

\seqsplit{javascript/HardcodedSecret} (severity 8) &
All services (JWT verification) &
Hardcoded \texttt{'defaultSecret'} fallback for \texttt{JWT\_SECRET} &
Removed fallback; services now require \texttt{JWT\_SECRET} env var and fail fast at startup if unset \\
\addlinespace

\seqsplit{javascript/UseCsurfForExpress} (severity 4) &
\seqsplit{iam-service}, \seqsplit{academic-service} &
No CSRF protection on cookie-authenticated mutating endpoints &
Added double-submit CSRF: non-\texttt{httpOnly} \texttt{XSRF-TOKEN} cookie issued on login/refresh, validated against \texttt{X-CSRF-Token} header on mutating requests (bearer-token requests exempted) \\
\addlinespace

\seqsplit{javascript/HTTPSourceWithUncheckedType} (severity 2, multiple) &
\seqsplit{academic-service/student.route.js}, \seqsplit{billing-service/payment.route.js} &
Unchecked type of \texttt{req.query.search} &
\seqsplit{express-validator}'s \seqsplit{query('search').optional().isString()} + \seqsplit{matchedData()} \\
\addlinespace

\seqsplit{javascript/HTTPSourceWithUncheckedType} (severity 2, $\times$4) &
\seqsplit{academic-service/teacher.route.js} (\seqsplit{/courses/stats}, \seqsplit{/courses/pending-grades}, \seqsplit{/courses/distribution}, \seqsplit{/courses/performance}) &
Unchecked \texttt{req.query.courseIds} passed to \texttt{.split(',')} &
Added \seqsplit{typeof courseIds !== 'string'} guard before \seqsplit{.split()} on all 4 endpoints \\
\addlinespace

\seqsplit{javascript/XSS} (unsanitized \texttt{req.params.id} reaching \texttt{res.write} via SSE hub) &
\seqsplit{iam-service/auth.controller.js} (\texttt{revokeSession}) &
\seqsplit{req.params.id} used unvalidated in a session-revoke flow whose result is broadcast over SSE &
Validate \seqsplit{req.params.id} against a UUID regex before use; reject with 400 if invalid \\
\addlinespace

\seqsplit{javascript/HTTPSourceWithUncheckedType} &
\seqsplit{iam-service/auth.controller.js} (\texttt{changePassword}) &
\seqsplit{req.body.newPassword.length} accessed without checking type &
\texttt{typeof} checks on \seqsplit{currentPassword}/\seqsplit{newPassword} before length validation \\
\addlinespace

Information disclosure &
All Express services &
\seqsplit{X-Powered-By} header exposed Express framework &
Disabled via \seqsplit{app.disable(\textquotesingle x-powered-by\textquotesingle)} \\
\addlinespace

Insecure crypto (dead-code taint) &
\texttt{auth.service.js} / JWT handling &
Unused \texttt{decodeToken} helper decoded JWTs without verifying signature &
Removed dead code \\
\addlinespace

Insecure hashing &
seed script &
\texttt{sha1}/\texttt{md5} used for demo session tokens and offline embedding hashes &
Replaced with \texttt{sha256} \\
\addlinespace

Cookie attribute static analysis &
\seqsplit{iam-service/auth.controller.js} &
\texttt{res.cookie} options built via a shared helper function (not statically verifiable by Snyk) &
Inlined \texttt{httpOnly}/\texttt{secure}/\texttt{sameSite} attributes at each \texttt{res.cookie()} call site \\

\bottomrule
\end{longtable}
